Research

Policy Implementation in Local Governance: A Case Study of Urban Development Programs

Final Stages of Research

In Progress

Abstract

This research examines the implementation challenges of urban development programs at the local government level, focusing on the gap between policy design and practical execution. Through qualitative case studies and quantitative analysis of program outcomes, the study identifies key institutional and administrative barriers to effective policy implementation and proposes a framework for improving governance processes.

Objectives

  • Analyze implementation gaps in urban development programs
  • Identify institutional barriers to effective policy execution
  • Develop recommendations for improved governance processes
